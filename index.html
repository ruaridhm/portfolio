About

I have always had an interest in computing. Growing up I was lucky enough to have access to obsolite computers from office upgrades through my fathers work. From this I learnt a lot about computer hardware and had my first introduction to programming through Qbasic and a bit later on Visual Basic.

Since then have dabbled in a few other programming languages with ranging levels of success. Generally just learning enough to complete the task at hand.

I have decided to take a more structured effort to learning Web Development. By working through various courses which are listed at the top of this page and by trying to gain a greater grasp of the key concepts and core elements to programming. This site has been created to document my learning progress, to showcase future projects and as a coding sandbox.

Because of this last purpose this site will change day by day as I implemment and debug new features and things I have learnt.
